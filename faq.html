Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 5D Memory Crystal technology, ordering, and data preservation.

How does 5D optical storage work?

A femtosecond laser creates self-assembled nanogratings inside fused silica glass. These nanostructures alter light in five dimensions — three spatial coordinates (x, y, z) plus two optical properties: slow axis orientation and retardance strength. This allows a single pit to encode eight bits (one byte) of information.

How much data can a crystal hold?

Current crystals store several terabytes per disc. Near-term capacity targets 20 TB, with an ultimate potential of 360 TB on a single 12cm diameter disc.

How long does the data last?

The estimated decay time is 1020 years at room temperature. At 190°C, crystal data remains stable for a period comparable to the age of the universe — approximately 13.8 billion years.

Can the data be erased or rewritten?

Yes. Unlike write-once media, 5D crystal data can be erased and rewritten, offering flexibility for archival workflows that require updates.

How durable is the crystal physically?

Crystals are made of fused quartz glass, which survives temperatures up to 1,000°C and direct impact of up to half a ton. They are resistant to EMP, radiation, chemical exposure, and electromagnetic interference.

How is data read from a crystal?

Data is retrieved using polarisation-sensitive microscopy combined with polarisers. The system analyses how light polarisation changes as it passes through the nanogratings to extract the encoded information.

Will future generations be able to read the crystals?

Yes. Visual instructions and holographic guides can be imprinted directly onto the crystal disc, providing future readers with the information needed to decode the data without requiring any external documentation.

How is this different from hard drives or cloud storage?

Hard drives fail within 5-10 years and require continuous power. Cloud storage depends on ongoing subscriptions and server infrastructure. Crystal requires zero energy to maintain, has no cyber attack surface, and needs no hardware refresh cycles.

Is crystal storage environmentally friendly?

Significantly. Data centres consume 1-2% of global electricity for cooling and maintenance. Crystal storage is write-once with zero ongoing energy requirement — no cooling, no servers, no carbon footprint for long-term storage.

Can I use crystal for crypto or seed phrase storage?

Yes. Crystal provides ideal cold storage for cryptocurrency seed phrases, wallet keys, and smart contract records. It is fully offline, unhackable, and permanent — eliminating the risks of hardware wallet failure or paper degradation.

Do you offer luxury or jewellery crystals?

Yes. We create bespoke crystal jewellery and commemorative luxury pieces with embedded data — personal messages, artworks, or authentication records sealed inside a wearable or display-quality crystal.

How do I order a crystal?

Visit our Order page, choose your crystal format and capacity, upload your data through our encrypted portal, and we handle the rest. Crystals are delivered in a presentation case.
